JOJI

George Kusunoki Miller

Timeline of Joji:

  • September 18, 1992 - George Miller was born in Osaka, Japan on September 18, 1992, to a Japanese father and an Australian mother.
  • June 15, 2008 - In 2008, Miller created the YouTube channel "DizaztaMusic" containing music and comedic content.
  • 2012 - He graduated from the international Canadian Academy in Kobe, Japan. He then moved to Brooklyn to complete his college education there.
  • January 22, 2013 - On January 22, 2013, Miller created the Youtube channel "TVFilthyFrank" to upload various rants and comedic content, where he was able to gain over 6 million subscribers.
  • February 2, 2013 - Miller uploaded a video to his Youtube channel titled "DO THE HARLEM SHAKE", a video of him and his friends playing around to a song, to which it gained over 61 million views and began the trend which took over Youtube in 2013, the Harlem Shake.
  • January 4, 2017 - Joji released his first full-length album, "Pink Season", consisting of 31 songs, under the name "Pink Guy" which was one of his alter egos during his TVFilthyFrank days.
  • November 3, 2017 - Joji released his first EP titled "In Tongues" consisting of 6 songs.
  • July 20, 2018 - Head in the Clouds is the debut compilation album by the record label 88rising. Joji was featured on 8 of 17 songs, including the title song "Head in the Clouds".
  • September 12, 2018 - Joji released the official music video for SLOW DANCING IN THE DARK which soon became a top hit and gained over 95 million views on Youtube and over 217 million streams on Spotify.
  • October 26, 2018 - BALLADS1 was Joji's debut studio album. BALLADS1 is primarily produced by Joji himself and features only one vocal feature, Trippie Redd.
  • June 14, 2019 - Joji released his single "Sanctuary" which received over 68 million streams on Spotify alone.
  • September 12, 2019 - Joji released another single featuring Don Krez.
  • September 25, 2020 - Nectar is the second studio album by Japanese singer-songwriter Joji, released on 25 September 2020 via 88rising. It features the singles "Sanctuary", "Run", "Gimme Love", "Daylight" with Diplo, and "Your Man", as well as collaborations with Benee, Lil Yachty, Omar Apollo, Yves Tumor and Rei Brown.
  • November 4, 2022 - Smithereens is the third studio album by Japanese singer-songwriter Joji, released on 4 November 2022 via 88rising and Warner Records. It was preceded by the singles "Glimpse of Us" and "Yukon". Joji is touring North America in promotion of the record from September 2022.
